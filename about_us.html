Hi! Thanks for coming here!

My name is Thiago Costa, I study Computer Engineering at Rose-Hulman
and am currently an exchange student at the 6-week JLP program
at the Kanazawa Institute of Technology in Japan.

This website was developed as a project for the Japanese communication class and aims to help beginners get interested and start their japanese
learning adventure through fun and interactive exercises, engaging them along the way. Learning Japanese is not an easy journey, but with consistent efforts it is possible to master the language.

I believe a big part of helping people learn something is
to make it a fun experience, so whether you are planning a visit to Japan, are interested in japanese literature, media or just
want to pass the time, this website has got you covered.

Enjoy your time learning a new language and let's start our adventure together!
